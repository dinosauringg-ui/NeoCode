How to Use
Saved Presets
Build a font, then hit Save to store it under a name. Use the dropdown to load any saved preset. Delete removes the currently loaded preset. Your presets are stored in your browser and persist between sessions.
Identity & Avatar
Enter your Neopets username — it appears in the live preview below exactly as it will on the boards. Click any unlocked avatar in the grid to set it as your active avatar. Use the search box to filter by name.
Board Code (neoHTML & neoSignature)
Paste your Neopets board font code into these fields. neoHTML is your post style — neoSignature is the text appended below every post. Use $C1–$C6 as color placeholders — the color extractor will fill these from your avatar automatically. The Live Preview renders exactly what your post will look like on the boards.
Avatar Checklist & Sync
Head to the Avatar Checklist tab. Paste the raw HTML source of your Neopets Neoboard Preferences page into the Sync field and hit Sync Avatars — it will automatically detect and mark all your unlocked avatars. You can also click any avatar manually to toggle it. Unlocked avatars appear in the Board Editor's avatar selector.
Color Picker
After selecting an avatar, a color extractor appears. Click COLOR on your avatar thumbnail to open a zoomed pixel picker — click any pixel to assign it to one of 6 color slots. Then hit Apply Extracted Colors to auto-fill your $C placeholders in the board code.
